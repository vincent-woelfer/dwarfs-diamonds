================== Global ==================
- world starts top-left (0,0)
- y increases downwards


================== CELL ==================
- cell starts top-left = (0,0) within cell
- cell has a grid-pos (Vector2i) 
- cell has a world-pos (Vector2) -> top-left of cell. But most of the times, cell-center (top-left + CellSize * 0.5) is used



================== DWARF ==================
- origin is at feet, centered horizontally
